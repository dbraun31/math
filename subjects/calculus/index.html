Calculus

Objective

I want to work towards a rich, deep understanding of calculus, which I perceive to be a large part of the underpinning of probability theory, statistics, and modeling.

I want to strike an ideal balance of breadth and depth. Derivatives and integrals are key (ie, function optimization). I anticipate I’ll balance between working thorough rigorous resources to establish a deeper, more thorough knowledge, and work through more surface-level resources if I need to more quickly get a working understanding of some of the concepts.
